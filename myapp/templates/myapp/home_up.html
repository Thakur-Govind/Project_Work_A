About The Farmer Portal

This portal is to help farmers to gain the utmost profit from their crops by directly conecting them to their customers and other pesticides,fertilizer suppliers.

Farmer has to register themselves and put all the crops they grow.

Customer has to register and whenever they want to buy a crop they can just search the farmers according to the crops and their state.

There is a section seller who supplies raw material like seeds,fertilizers and pesticides.Farmer can directly buy these raw materials.
